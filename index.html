Meet Chase

Most Contractors Have a Business.
Chase Has a Calling.

At 9 years old, while his dad was at the hardware store picking up spare parts, Chase rewired the family garbage disposal. By himself. His dad came home to find it working perfectly and a kid covered in grease with the biggest grin you've ever seen.

That was the moment. Chase hasn't put a tool down since.

What started as a kid who couldn't stop fixing things became a career built on one core belief: the small details are what separate good from unforgettable.

"I pride myself on my detail-oriented nature, because I know that the fine details are what make a project shine and stand out from those like it."

— Chase Jack, Owner & Founder

Today, The Jack of All Trades serves homeowners across Indianapolis, Fishers, Carmel, Noblesville, and all of Hamilton County.
