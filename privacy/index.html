Privacy Policy

The Jack of All Trades — Effective April 28, 2026

The Jack of All Trades ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at gottacalljack.com or interact with our business.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address
  • Project Information: Details about your home improvement project submitted through our contact form or during phone conversations
  • Communication Records: Records of calls, text messages, and emails between you and our team

We may also automatically collect basic website usage data such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and project requests
  • Schedule estimates and appointments
  • Send project updates, estimate follow-ups, and appointment reminders via SMS text message and email
  • Request feedback and reviews after project completion
  • Send occasional service-related communications, including seasonal offers and referral opportunities
  • Improve our website and services

Text Messaging (SMS)

By providing your phone number and opting in through our website contact form, you consent to receive text messages from The Jack of All Trades. These messages may include:

  • Responses to your inquiries
  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Estimate follow-ups
  • Review requests after project completion
  • Referral program information
  • Seasonal service offers

Message frequency varies. Typically 1–6 messages per project engagement.

Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier's standard messaging and data rates apply to any messages you send or receive.

You can opt out at any time. Reply STOP to any text message to unsubscribe from future messages. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

You can opt back in at any time by texting START or by contacting us directly.

For help, reply HELP to any text message or call us at the number listed below.

We will never sell, rent, or share your phone number with third-party marketers.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use tools such as GoHighLevel (CRM and communications), Google Business Profile, and website hosting services to operate our business. These providers may process your data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or court order.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your personal information from our records
  • Opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
